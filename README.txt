Application Structure

The app consists of 5 main pages with an approve proof page and download page

The first 5 form pages are called steps and have pagination. They Include:

1. Your Information
2. Select a Template
3. Basic tournament Information
4. Tournament Description
5. Tournament Contact Information

Biggest Factor: Routing

Main Content Box is split up into Left and Right sides. The left contains the Forms that
will be cycled through. The right a presentation of the Poster. When a user enters their 
tournament information it should update on the right side. The presentation of the poster
is viewable on all pages except the last.

The Header, Middle and Footer will be the only sections in the index.html

Your Information is the Base Url
Select a template will be /select-
